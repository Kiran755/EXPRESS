What is an MUN?

1. Why MUN? - Helps students develop leadership skills, research, writing, public speaking, and problem-solving skills. 2. Why is it important? We can do the same stuff in GDs and debates too! - Coming up with solutions that are acceptable to a majority of the representatives, abiding by a given set if rules, inculcates skills of negotiation, conflict resolution, and cooperation.
